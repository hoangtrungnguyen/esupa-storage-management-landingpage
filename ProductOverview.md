# Product Overview: Store Management Software for Manufacturing

Our product, "Idea Space Board", is a comprehensive store management software designed specifically for the manufacturing industry.

It helps you visualize every step of your manufacturing process, from raw materials to finished goods. By providing a shared visual platform, it enhances collaboration between your procurement, production, and sales teams.

Key features include:
- Inventory tracking and management
- Production process visualization
- Supply chain management
- Sales and order tracking

With "Idea Space Board", you can streamline your operations, reduce costs, and improve efficiency across your entire manufacturing workflow.
